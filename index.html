About Us

Having built up a very solid and stable reputation for our quality products and services over the last two
decades, the Real Computer Shop is the perfect choice, as we offer unrivalled expertise for all your IT
Solutions and we are proud to have assisted thousands of customers since opening our first store back in
January 2015.

In 2017 we formed a dedicated business team delivering bespoke solutions across Derbyshire and the greater
Yorkshire region. Our second store opened in 2018 in Kurunegala, Kandy, again offering our IT retail
expertise to a much wider audience.

So, whether you’re a home user or a large nationwide organisation, we take the time to understand you and
your requirements, thus helping you achieve your objectives through the provision of IT.

In our stores you will find an array of hardware, software and all associated peripherals to fulfil your
needs, backed up by friendly, helpful and expert staff. Both stores have a technical repair workshop
where we carry out thousands of repairs each year.

MSI is a gaming brand that has been around for quite some time. The company has a reputation for making high-quality, high-performance products. Browse the... top-ranked list of MSI computers below along with associated reviews and opinions.

DELL

Dell's laptops look great, offer decent internal hardware, and have heaps of style (and they have some of the best ruggedized options on the market). They're made with premium materials, sport a solid battery life, and are light and portable.

MAC

Apple is a prominent hardware and software company best known for its series of personal computers, the iPod and its innovative marketing strategies for its products. Introduced in 1984, the Macintosh was the first widely sold personal computer with a graphical user interface (GUI).
